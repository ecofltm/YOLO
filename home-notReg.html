About YOLO

YOLO, founded in 2024, is your ultimate destination for flavorful and nutritious frozen yogurt. We pride ourselves on offering a wide selection of creamy yogurt flavors crafted from premium natural ingredients. At YOLO, you can personalize your dessert with fresh fruits, crunchy nuts, and a variety of indulgent toppings at our self-serve stations. Committed to sustainability, we prioritize local sourcing and eco-friendly practices to ensure every visit to YOLO is both delicious and environmentally conscious.
